My First Week At AkiraChix

Akirachix is a dream come for every poor girl child who wants to have a great life.My experience is both good and bad as explained below.

Good Things

  1. The aluminies are great in their teaching.
  2. The water used for showering is warm and healthy
  3. The environment is very quiet and good for studies

Bad Things

  • The electricity sometimes goes off and we have no power to use for our activities
  • The cook serving food is alone sometimes and so time is wasted serving very many students,so we can't make it to class in time
